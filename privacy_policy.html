1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We collect personal information that you provide directly to us when you register for and use our services, including:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, national ID number, and other identification documents
  • Profile Information: Profile pictures, contact details, and other information you choose to provide

1.2 Financial Information

To provide our financial management services, we collect:

  • Savings account information and transaction history
  • Loan application and repayment information
  • Share ownership and dividend information
  • Payment and transaction records
  • Meeting attendance records
  • Fines and expense records

1.3 Device and Technical Information

When you use our App, we automatically collect certain information about your device, including:

  • Device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers
  • IP address and network information
  • App usage data, including features accessed and pages visited
  • Crash reports and performance data

1.4 Permissions and Data Access

Our App requests the following permissions to provide core functionality:

  • Internet & Network: Required for API calls, data synchronization, and online services
  • Phone & SMS: Used for USSD transactions, SMS verification, and payment processing
  • Storage: Required for downloading and exporting financial reports, documents, and data
  • Camera: Used for capturing profile pictures and scanning identification documents
  • Location: Used for branch services, location-based features, and fraud prevention
  • Notifications: Used to send you important updates about your account, transactions, and meetings
  • Vibration: Used for notification alerts

  • To protect against fraud, unauthorized access, and other security threats

  • To ensure the security and integrity of our services

2.4 Legal Compliance

  • To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes
  • To respond to government requests and court orders
  • To enforce our terms of service and other agreements

2.5 Service Improvement

  • To analyze usage patterns and improve our App's functionality
